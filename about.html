About Us

Empowered Women, United for Maternal Health.

We are an NGO borne from a mother's love for her child, and a determination to give her child the very best by providing mothers with the highest quality,science-based fitness solutions that we believe every mother deserves.

Hack-her-Squad, Founders

This program was initiated by a group of passionate women who came together with a shared purpose: to champion the cause of maternal health and empower pregnant women and teenage mothers. We firmly believe that every woman deserves access to quality care, support, and resources during the transformative period in their lives.

What We Do

Learn More About What We Do And Get Involved

Pregnancy Education and Counseling

At Tiny Steps, we are committed to providing comprehensive pregnancy education and counseling to individuals, particularly teens and adults. We believe that knowledge is empowering, and by offering accurate and evidence-based information, we can help individuals make informed decisions about their health and well-being during pregnancy. Our team of healthcare professionals and counselors work together to offer personalized prenatal care, nutritional guidance, and emotional support to pregnant individuals.


Learn More

Fitness and Wellness Programs

We recognize the benefits of maintaining fitness during pregnancy. Our website offers a range of safe and effective exercise programs designed specifically for pregnant women. Our fitness programs are developed by certified professionals and take into account the specific needs and limitations of pregnant individuals. We provide step-by-step guidance, exercise modifications for each trimester, and tips for incorporating fitness into daily routines. Through safe and effective exercises, we aim to improve strength, flexibility, and overall well-being.

Learn More

Nutrition Guidance for Maternal & Fetal Health

Proper nutrition is crucial for the health and development of both mother and baby during pregnancy. We offer comprehensive nutrition guidance to ensure optimal maternal and fetal health. Our platform provides valuable information on balanced diets, essential nutrients, healthy weight gain, and managing common pregnancy-related condition. By promoting healthy eating habits, we aim to reduce the risk of complications and contribute to the overall well-being of mothers and children.



Learn More

Accessible Maternal Healthcare Services

We are dedicated to improving access to high-quality maternal healthcare services for all individuals. We understand the barriers and challenges that pregnant individuals may face, including financial constraints, cultural considerations, or geographical limitations. Through our platform, we strive to provide accessible resources and connect individuals with healthcare providers who offer empathetic and culturally sensitive care. We believe that by improving access and promoting patient-centered care, we can contribute to reducing maternal and child mortality rates.

Learn More

Collaborations and Partnerships

At Tiny Steps, We believe that impactful change can be achieved through collaborations and partnerships. We actively seek collaborations with organizations, healthcare providers, government agencies, and other stakeholders who share our vision of reducing maternal and child mortality rates. By pooling our resources, expertise, and influence, we can create a more significant and sustainable impact. We collaborate on research projects, advocacy campaigns, and policy initiatives to improve maternal and child health outcomes.



Learn More

Outreach and Awareness Programs

Creating awareness and fostering a supportive community are essential elements in reducing maternal and child mortality rates. At Tiny Steps, we actively engage in community outreach initiatives to raise awareness about teen/adult pregnancy care and fitness. We organize workshops, webinars, and events to educate individuals, families, and community organizations about the importance of prenatal care, healthy lifestyles, and available support systems. Through these initiatives, we aim to debunk myths, reduce stigma, and encourage open conversations surrounding pregnancy and maternal health.
