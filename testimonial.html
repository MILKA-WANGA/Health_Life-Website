Testimonial

Giving Hope to Teen Mothers and Saving Pregnant Women

As a woman raised in Uganda, My mother claimed to know so much about childbirth and motherhood, after having my first child I went through postpartum depression which my own 55 year old mother couldn’t understand but after a friend recommended this website to me I went through all the programs and all the fitness trainings I benefited so much. TinySteps has helped me through the hardest stage of motherhood and I’m so thankful and grateful.

29, Afiya Abothi
New Mum with PPD

As a teenage orphan, navigating the journey of pregnancy was an overwhelming experience for me. I felt lost and scared, unsure of where to turn for support. That's when I discovered Tinysteps, and they became my lifeline. The team at Tinysteps welcomed me with open arms, providing me with the care, resources, and guidance I desperately needed. They not only ensured my physical well-being but also supported my emotional and mental health throughout the entire process. The team offered me scholarship to complete my secondary education and enrolled me for a vocational training. Thanks to Tinysteps, I felt empowered and prepared for motherhood, and I can confidently say that their unwavering support played a crucial role in helping me overcome the challenges I faced. I am forever grateful for their dedication to reducing maternal mortality and for giving me hope for a brighter future for myself and my child.

15, Samantha Phiri
Teen mom

TinySteps has taught me so much and for that i'll always recommend them to all moms.
